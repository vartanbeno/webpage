Front End Developer Intern - Summer 2018

Ottawa, Montreal, Toronto, Waterloo

Shopify internships are 4 month, paid internships and are open to students, co-ops, recent graduates and those early on in their careers. The summer internship will run May 7th - August 24th 2018.

We will be accepting applications for the Front End Development Internship until 12:00 PM EST on Friday January 12th 2018 and you can expect to hear back from us about the status of your application by Monday January 22nd.

*If you are looking for an internship in another discipline, keep an eye out on our careers site as more postings for summer 2018 will open in the new year.*

Who are we?

The Shopify platform hosts over 500,000 active online stores around the world, ranging from shops run by small entrepreneurs, to big names like Tesla Motors, Herschel, and the LA Lakers. We want to make commerce better for everyone and we’re helping thousands of merchants achieve their dream of running a successful business!

Front End devs at Shopify specialize in using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create the unique interfaces our users have come to expect. They work closely with designers and application developers to help steer design concepts and build them in a maintainable, scalable way within Shopify's technical architecture. You can learn more about what our teams are up to on our Eng blog!

Who are we looking for?

Schooling and years of experience are not a requirement here at Shopify. We feel that the best way to learn is not always in the typical classroom, but through practical experience, hard work, and passion. If you love challenging yourself with projects, hacking just for fun or learning new languages and development techniques, then keep reading!

Life at Shopify

Being an intern at Shopify means you get to make an impact and get shit done. You’ll get to work along side full time devs, deploying to master on real projects and features that will directly affect our merchants. Find out what past interns have had to say here. For more stories from Inside Shopify, check out our blog.

In your application

We want to learn about your passion for front end development and initiative beyond school requirements.  Do you: get involved in your student associations/clubs; hack on your own projects; organize events, attend mentoring sessions or meet ups; build at hackathons; contribute to open source projects; seek out challenges and learning opportunities? If you do, tell us about it in your cover letter! You can address it to Lauren.

Experience comes in many forms, many skills are transferable, and passion goes a long way. If your experience is close to what we’re looking for, consider applying. We know that diversity of thought makes for the best problem-solving and creative thinking, which is why we're dedicated to adding new perspectives to the team and encourage everyone to apply.

Before hitting "Apply"

Help us learn more about you! Please complete each of the following tasks. (Answer fields provided on application page)

1. Learn more about the product you could be helping to build! Create a free trial Shopify store to get familiar. Have fun and be creative!

2. Try out this Front End Development challenge and enter the link of your submission in the answer field.

3. Each quarter at Shopify we hold company-wide Hack Days. Watch the video, then pitch us an idea for what you would work on, how you'd build it, and why!
